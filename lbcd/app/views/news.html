Forum Origin, Diversity and Territories 2014 will take place in Turin, Italy, from 21-23 October 2014. This year, the forum will highlight the role of family farming in the bio-cultural diversities conservation as well in the interactions within origin's promotion. The Forum is organized by Diversity & Development (international association based in Montpellier, France), FiBl (Research Institute of Organic Agriculture in Swiss) and their partners FAO, ONUDI, Slow Food International, CIRAD, RIMISP DTR-IC Program, the Platform Diversity Biocultural & Territories and REDD, All information available on the web site: http://origin-for-sustainability.org/en/
For more information: info@origin-for-sustainability.org
